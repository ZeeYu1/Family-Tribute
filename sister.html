Sister

My Sister

My little sister is such an emotional soul—anything that stirs up strong feelings can bring her to tears. Because of this, I feel both happy and worried. As your brother, I worry that your sensitivity might cause you pain or trouble at times. But at the same time, I’m also happy because you have something I don’t—a deep sense of empathy and the ability to express your emotions freely.

When we travel and see beautiful places, I often just give a quiet smile, while you light up like a bundle of joy, filling the moment with laughter and excitement. You bring so much happiness into my life, like a ray of sunshine.

I wish you all the happiness during your college years! This is one of the most colorful, carefree times of your life, so make the most of it and enjoy every moment. With all my love, your brother.
