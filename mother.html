Mother

My Mother

My mom is a lot like my dad in many ways, but with one key difference—she’s much calmer, with a temperament that is less prone to emotional highs and lows. That said, I do want to call out one flaw, especially from when I was younger: you used to always peek at my things, and that drove me crazy. I’ve always valued my personal privacy, so this habit of yours made me quite angry and frustrated back then. But just like my dad, my emotions come and go quickly, and all of that is in the past now.

Now, you support every decision I make, letting me take charge of my own life. Whether it’s where I go, what I do, or which certifications I pursue, you’ve always given me the freedom to choose. You’ve become more like a wise advisor—a consultant guiding me through the early stages of my journey. Thank you, Mom, for being that pillar of wisdom. I love you!
