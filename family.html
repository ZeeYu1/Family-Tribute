Our Family

My parents aren’t the type to openly express their love, but I know it’s always there. My dad is more outgoing and his emotions are easier to read—he makes sure we know how much he cares through both his words and actions. My mom, on the other hand, is more reserved. She doesn’t always express her feelings directly, but her love shows in the countless little things she does for our family every day. Both of them, in their own ways, have taught me what love truly means—steady, unconditional, and always present, even without words.
